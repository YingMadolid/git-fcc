About Dr. Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist known as the "Father of the Green Revolution." Through his research and development of high-yield crop varieties, he helped improve food production around the world and saved millions of lives from famine.

In 1970, he received the Nobel Peace Prize for his contributions to increasing the global food supply and promoting agricultural development.
